Dr. Greger's Daily Dozen
Most of us might know, that eating healthy is a good idea. But what kind of food actually IS healthy? And how can we find our way through this massive jungle of information concerning food and nutrition? Well, thankfully there are people out there who are creating a path that we can take.
For example Dr. Michael Greger, who has looked through hundreds of scientific studies to filter out the relevant information we need to live a healthier and happier life while eating delicious food. In the end he came up with this list of a dozen foodgroups that we should eat on a daily basis to get the maximum effect of a well balanced and healthy nutritional intake.
Beans
Servings: 3 per day
chickpeas, lentils, kidneybeans, black beans, peas, butter beans, cannellini beans soyabeans, tofu etc.
Berries
Servings: 1 per day
blueberries, raspberries, strawberries, blackberries, cherries, cranberries etc.
Other Fruits
Servings: 3 per day
apples, bananas, oranges, melon, lemons, plums, pears, clementines, kiwi etc.
Cruciferous Vegetables
Servings: 1 per day
broccoli, cauliflower, kale, bok choi, cabbage, brussels sprouts, horseradish etc.
Greens
Servings: 2 per day
kale, spinach, swiss chard, spring greens, rocket, salad greens etc.
Other Vegetables
Servings: 2 per day
carrots, tomatoes, pumpkin, sweet potatoes, garlic, beetroot, courgettes, mushrooms, onions, etc.
Flaxseeds
Servings: 1 per day
one table spoon of ground up flaxseeds a day
Nuts & Seeds
Servings: 1 per day
walnuts, cashew, almonds, brazil nuts, sunflower seeds, pumpkin seeds, chias seeds, pistachios etc.
Herbs & Spices
Servings: 1 per day
a quarter of a teaspoon of turmeric, plus any others you love: basil, cardamom, cinnamon, coriander, cumin, dill, fenugreek, ginger, garlic, majoran, mustard powder, nutmeg, oregano, smoked paprika, parsley, pepper, peppermint, rosemary, saffon, sage, thyme, vanilla etc.
Whole Grains
Servings: 3 per day
oats, whole wheat pasta, brown rice, buckwheat, teff, millet, quinoa etc.
Drinks
Servings: 5 per day
water, green tea, hibiscus tea, black tea, matcha tea, peppermint tea, coffee etc.
Exercise
Servings: 1 per day
ideally 90 minutes a day of moderate activity, such as walking
